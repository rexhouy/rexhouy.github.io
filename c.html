Addressing

Consider memory as an array, M[x] is the xth char of memory. Use the following method to represent an address.

formatoperand
$immimmConstant
ER[E]Value of the register

(E)M[R[E]]

(Ea, Eb)M(R[Ea] + R[Eb])

Move operand

b-char, w-word, l-double word

movbmovb S DMove char from S to D
movwmovw $0x104 %axMove word 0x104 -> %ax
movlmovl %eax 0x1Move double word R[%eax] -> 0x1
movs较小数据复制到一个较大的数据位置，高位用符号位拓展。对应的指令为movsbw, movsbl, movswl
movz同上，高位用0拓展,movzbw, movzbl, movzwl
pushlpushl %eax%epx用于保存栈顶指针，该指令将会把%epx中的值-4（新的栈顶位置）,然后将R[%eax]保存到M[R[%epx]]
poplpopl %eax将M[R[%epx]]保存到%eax，然后将%epx中的值+4
所有栈操作都在栈顶进行，栈底在高字节，栈顶在低字节，所以压栈时栈顶指针要-4. * 反汇编
